ABOUT US

Home to 46-bay ,multi-tier ,floodlit driving range,powered by Toptracer Range technology . Complimented by a practice green and bunker ,coffee shop and Indian Golf store .There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TopRacer Range

Our Toptracer Range is the most advanced technology in the industry. It offers a fun, engaging, tech-driven experience that appeals to seasoned players, range rivals, friends, family members, and even first-time golfers. The technology provides instant shot replays and statistical feedback on interactive game screens in your hitting bay,

Adventure Golf

Our Adventure Golf is a fun and exciting activity for all ages. Our two 18-hole dinosaur themed courses are perfect for families, friends and work colleagues. We also offer children's birthday parties and corporate events. Our courses are open 7 days a week and we look forward to welcoming you to our club.

Golf Lessons

Our PGA professionals offer a range of golf lessons for all ages and abilities. We offer individual and group lessons, as well as junior coaching and ladies group lessons. Our lessons are available 7 days a week and we look forward to welcoming you to our club.
